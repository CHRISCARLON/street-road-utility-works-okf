# Reading

Long-form background notes for the MUDDI bundle — the source standards and the
reasoning that connects the conceptual model to its UK Excavation Profile. Unlike
the rest of the bundle (generated class, feature-type, codelist and relationship
pages), these are hand-written explainers.

* [MUDDI Conceptual Model](muddi-conceptual-model.md) - the OGC 23-024 standard on its own: the abstract class hierarchy, attributes, associations and graph topology.
* [MUDDI → UK Excavation Profile: How the Bridge Works](muddi-uk-excavation-bridge.md) - how the UK profile is derived from and extends the conceptual model, and why each change is permitted.
* [MUDDI UK Excavation Profile — Deep Dive](uk-excavation-profile.md) - the concrete UK profile in full: schemas, codelists, the nine utility types, flattened inheritance and UK-specific attributes.
